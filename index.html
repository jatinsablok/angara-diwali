About Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, is one of the most significant celebrations in Hindu culture. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, knowledge over ignorance, and good over evil.

During this auspicious time, families come together to light diyas, share sweets, and create beautiful rangoli patterns. The warm glow of countless lamps illuminates homes and hearts, spreading joy and prosperity throughout the community.

This festival represents the triumph of righteousness and the renewal of life, bringing families and communities together in celebration of hope, love, and spiritual enlightenment.
